My Projects

Personal Rail Passenger App

After using many apps for planning my rail journeys, I realised there was not one app that tailoured journeys towards the indidual's needs for regularly travel between their stations of use. Not only this, but most apps out there did not display what services were regularly delayed so that users could plan their journey's around expected late trains. An app was therefore designed where the user's specific train routes and destinations can be stored within the app, along with popular schedules that they use. The user is then able to look at the app's home screen which details departures happening now from their favourite stations and whether they are late. Train punctualilty is also included historically in another section, so that users can expect delays from certain trains on certain schedules.
