Northcoders News

As part of my studies undertaken at Northcoders, I completed two sprints that contributed to my final portfolio. These two sprints were based on a Reddit style news aggregation website with web content rating and discussion forums, which demonstrated my understanding of what I had learnt on the course.

Technical Details

The database used for this was the non-relational database MongoDB, with Mongoose being used for integrating the schema with the database. MongoDB was specified for this project as the data for social media sites tends not to confirm to any relationship between different sets of data.
